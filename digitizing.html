Digitizing

Digitizing is the process of converting any design, logo, or art into an embroidery design that can be understood by the embroidery machine. Digitizing process needs to be very efficient and purposeful so it can produce perfect results. Our award-winning and skilled digitizers can turn any artwork into digitizing with excellent quality. Our professional embroidery digitization team can do perfect digitization that can meet your expectations. Each design is punched by our professional digitizers and goes through rigorous quality checks to ensure that the design you receive is of highest possible quality. Galaxy Punching ensures faster turnaround to provide you the files on time. We have different professional digitizers for different embroidery digitizing software to meet the requirements of the design and embroidery machine.

If you are looking for digitizing then you have come to the right place.
