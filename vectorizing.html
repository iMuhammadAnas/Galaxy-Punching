Vectorizing

Vectorizing is the process of redrawing images from raster into vector forms by cleaning up poor quality images without losing their quality. Vectorizing is suitable for color separation, custom logo, graphic design, and computer animation for creating smoothly-shaded drawing with rich colors and different styles. Galaxy Punching has a great vision to transform the digital art world by providing vector art with excellent quality in the most affordable rates in the market. Our Galaxy team can make vector artwork of any image within few hours. We use latest software with utmost dedication skills to create vector graphics from any raster images or rough sketches. We provide vectors files in pdf, ai, eps, jpg and in many other formats upon request.

Raster to Vector

Raster image contains thousands of individual pixels in them and it undermines the picture quality. To counter that, vectorizing process is used to clear the image and avoid the undermined quality. Vector images are very good for the purpose of screen printing, engraving, signs, company logos, business cards, t-shirt logos etc. Vector images can be resized to any size without losing the picture quality.
